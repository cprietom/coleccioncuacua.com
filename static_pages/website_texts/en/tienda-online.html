All Colección Cuacuá tales are bilingual, illustrated, in verse and ideal for children of all ages.
- From 0 to 5 years old they'll have a blast listening to their parents quack like a tiger or roar like a squirrel.
- From 6 years they will practice their reading skills in english and spanish.
- From 9 years they will learn new vocabulary in english and spanish.
- All of them will enjoy the rhymes in both languages.
