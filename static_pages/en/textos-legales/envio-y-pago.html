Shipping and payment

Products

Cuacuá Collection sells products for which detailed information is given, including a photograph of them, description, name and price. Product Prices.

Precios de los productos

The products that appear in the Cuacuá Collection do so with the price in Euros including VAT and excluding shipping costs.

Prices may vary without notice and at any time.

The price of the items will not be considered any contractual commitment and will be considered as a purchase proposal to place the order. For this reason, if there is an error in the price or if it has been modified, the customer will be duly informed and confirmation will be requested to proceed with the order.

Order Preparation

As a general rule, in 1-2 business days the order is processed and ready to ship. From that moment, deliveries normally occur in 3-4 business days through national courier. In case there is no availability of stock or any problem with the shipment we will contact the customer informing him of the delay and the estimated delivery date.

Cuacuá Collection makes every effort to respect the delivery deadlines mentioned on the Web. However, Cuacuá Collection is not responsible for the consequences due to a delay in shipping or a loss of the package by yourself or for unforeseeable or insurmountable reasons or causes of a force majeure.

Shipping policy at Christmas and special dates (August, national holidays, the Community of Madrid ...)

On these dates the orders will have a longer preparation time than usual. However, they can be made and the buyer will be informed about delivery times.

Shipping costs

Except offer or promotion, these are the shipping costs that will be applied in orders of up to 20 copies. For orders of more than 20 copies, please contact the Cuacuá Collection at pedidos@coleccioncuacua.com

  • Destination Peninsula: 4,03€ plus IVA
  • Destination Baleares, Ceuta and Melilla: 5,77€ plus IVA
  • Destination Canary Islands: 10,12€ plus IVA
  • International shipments: From 23,19€ plus IVA (depending on country)
Shipping costs include handling and packaging costs, as well as postal costs.

The cost of the indicated shipping costs will not be considered any contractual commitment and will be considered as a proposal to place the order. For this reason, if there is an error in the amount or if it has been modified, the client will be duly informed and confirmation will be requested to proceed with the order.
